Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know about Kolay.

Kolay is open to everyone. Go to app.learnkolay.com (or click Go to the app above), create a free account and start right away.
The Free Plan is always free: unlimited lessons and flashcards. The Pro Plan unlocks reading texts, reference grammar, and more conversations with the AI, and costs €3.99 a month or €29.99 a year. Full details on the Pricing page.
The Free Plan includes 3 messages per day with the AI tutor, enough to try out how it works. The Pro Plan bumps that up to 70 messages per week: the limit is weekly, not daily, on purpose, so you can concentrate them into a few sessions instead of being forced to spread them out every day, and have longer, more personalized conversations when you really need to practice.
It's a system that decides when to review each word based on how well you remember it. If you remember a word well, you review it after more days. If you forget it, you review it sooner. That way you don't waste time reviewing things you already know, and you don't forget the hard ones.
No. Kolay is a web app: it opens in your browser, on any device, no App Store or Play Store needed.

On iPhone (Safari): open the app, tap the Share button (□↑), then "Add to Home Screen." It behaves like a native, full-screen app.

On Android (Chrome): open the app, tap the three dots in the top right, then "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app." From then on it opens like a regular app.
Duolingo is built to bring you back every day with points, streaks and notifications. Kolay is built to make you actually learn: a structured path by level (A1 to B1), grammar built into the lessons, serious spaced repetition. No empty gamification.
Turkish is already available, and Swedish is in the works (coming August 2026). You can pick your language directly in the app after signing up. We're collecting suggestions for the next ones: write to us from the Languages page.
We use Supabase for authentication and saving your progress. Your data is never shared or sold to third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy.
