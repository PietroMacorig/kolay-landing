3. Recipients and transfers outside the EU

To provide the service, the Data Controller uses the following sub-processors, who process data on its behalf:

  • Supabase Inc. (USA), authentication and progress database. EU→USA transfer via Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46 GDPR).
  • Vercel Inc. (USA), hosting of the application and landing page, technical logs, and Vercel Web Analytics (aggregated traffic statistics, without cookies). Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46 GDPR).
  • Groq Inc. (USA), AI processing of messages sent to the Speaking feature, to generate the tutor's response in real time. Under contract (Groq Services Agreement), Groq does not use this data to train its models and does not retain it beyond processing, except for temporary logs of up to 30 days for platform security and reliability. Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46 GDPR).
  • Formspree Inc. (USA), receiving emails sent through the landing page forms (language suggestion, FAQ questions). Standard Contractual Clauses (Art. 46 GDPR).
  • OVH SAS (France, EU), management of the hello@learnkolay.com mailbox used for user support. Data processed entirely within the European Union, no transfer outside the EU.

Data is not transferred to third parties for their own purposes.

4. Retention period

  • Account data and study progress: kept for as long as the account is active. After deletion: erased within 30 days.
  • Technical data and logs: maximum 30 days.
  • AI message counter: kept for as long as the account is active, to enforce the plan's weekly/daily limits.
  • Emails and messages sent through the landing page forms: kept on Formspree according to their retention policy, deletable on request by writing to hello@learnkolay.com.
  • Aggregated traffic data (Vercel Web Analytics): since it cannot be traced back to an individual user, it does not fall within the notion of personal data retained individually; the available history window depends on the Vercel plan in use.

5. Rights of the data subject

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR): obtain confirmation of the processing and a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): request the deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR): restrict processing in certain cases provided by law.
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR): receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection (Art. 21 GDPR): object to processing based on legitimate interest.

To exercise your rights, write to hello@learnkolay.com. We will respond within 30 days of receiving the request (Art. 12 GDPR).

7. Cookies

Kolay uses exclusively strictly necessary technical cookies for the service to function (authenticated session management). No profiling cookies, tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies are used.

Landing page traffic statistics are collected via Vercel Web Analytics, a service that by nature does not use cookies or any identifier that would allow a visitor to be recognized across visits.

Since there are no non-technical cookies or tracking tools, a cookie banner is not required under applicable regulations (ePrivacy Directive, Italian Garante's cookie guidelines).

If the Data Controller becomes aware that data of children under 14 has been collected without parental or guardian consent, it will proceed with its immediate deletion.
